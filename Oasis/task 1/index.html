Featured Games

Devil May Cry 5

"Devil May Cry 5" takes place several years after the events of the previous game and follows the story of Dante, the demon hunter, and his companions Nero and V. The game features a thrilling storyline that revolves around the invasion of the demonic forces in the city of Red Grave. The demon hunter trio must fight against hordes of demons and powerful bosses to save the world from destruction.

God of War-Ragnarok

"God of War Ragnarok" is an upcoming action-adventure video game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the sequel to the critically acclaimed "God of War" game released in 2018."God of War Ragnarok" continues the story of Kratos, the Spartan warrior turned Norse god, and his son Atreus. The game takes place in the Norse mythology setting, where Kratos and Atreus must navigate a world filled with gods, monsters, and mythical creatures.

Dead by Daylight

"Dead by Daylight" is a multiplayer survival horror game developed by Behaviour Interactive. It offers a unique and intense experience where players can take on the roles of both survivors and killers in thrilling asymmetrical matches. Here's a description of the game: In "Dead by Daylight," players can choose to play as one of the survivors or the killer. The survivors, typically four in a match, must work together to escape a nightmarish environment and avoid being caught and sacrificed by the killer. The objective is to repair a set number of generators scattered throughout the map to power the exit gates and make their escape.
